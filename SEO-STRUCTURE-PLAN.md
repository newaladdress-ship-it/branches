# SEO SILLO STRUCTURE PLAN

## Current URL Structure:
- / (Homepage)
- /categories
- /categories/[category]
- /add-business
- /about
- /contact

## NEW OPTIMIZED URL STRUCTURE:

### Primary Navigation (Tier 1)
- / (Homepage)
- /categories/ (Category hub)
- /locations/ (Location hub)
- /add-business (Conversion page)

### Category Silos (Tier 2-3)
- /categories/[category-name]/ (Main category page)
- /categories/[category-name]/[sub-category]/ (Sub-category pages)
- /categories/[category-name]/[city]/ (Category + City pages)

### Location Silos (Tier 2-3)
- /locations/[city]/ (Main city page)
- /locations/[city]/[category]/ (City + Category pages)
- /locations/[city]/[category]/[subcategory]/ (City + Category + Subcategory)

### Business Pages (Tier 4)
- /business/[business-name]/ (Individual business listings)

### Content Landing Pages (Tier 2-3)
- /best-[category]-in-[city]/ (Long-tail keyword pages)
- /top-[business-type]-near-me/ (Near-me searches)
- /[category]-guide-[city]/ (Category guides by city)

## URL Examples:
- /categories/restaurants/
- /categories/restaurants/karachi/
- /locations/karachi/
- /locations/karachi/restaurants/
- /business/abc-restaurant-karachi/
- /best-restaurants-in-karachi/
- /top-doctors-near-me/

## Priority Implementation Order:
1. Create location-based pages (/locations/[city]/)
2. Add category + city combinations
3. Create individual business pages
4. Build long-tail landing pages
