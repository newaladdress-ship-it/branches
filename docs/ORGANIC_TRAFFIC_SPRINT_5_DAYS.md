# Organic Traffic Sprint (5 Days)

This sprint is designed to maximize search visibility quickly for directory websites.

## Day 1: Technical + Indexability

- Deploy latest code.
- Confirm 301 redirects work for legacy URLs.
- Submit sitemap in Google Search Console.
- Request indexing for:
  - Home page
  - Top 12 category pages
  - Top 30 location-category pages
  - Top 50 business pages

## Day 2: Freshness + Content Signals

- Publish 50-100 new or updated business listings.
- Ensure each listing has:
  - unique description (not duplicate)
  - category + city
  - direct phone number
- Add internal links from homepage to highest-priority categories.

## Day 3: CTR Optimization

- Review pages with impressions but low CTR in Search Console.
- Rewrite title/meta for top 20 low-CTR URLs with intent-first wording:
  - "Best [Category] in [City]"
  - "Top [Category] Services in [City]"
  - "Find Verified [Category] in [City]"

## Day 4: Entity + GEO SEO

- Expand FAQ on top category/location pages.
- Ensure JSON-LD present:
  - BreadcrumbList
  - ItemList
  - FAQPage
  - LocalBusiness on business pages
- Keep answers concise and factual for AI search extraction.

## Day 5: Scale Winners

- Identify top 5 cities with highest impressions.
- Publish 30 additional city-category pages or refresh existing ones.
- Build more internal links toward top-performing pages from:
  - homepage sections
  - category pages
  - related business blocks

## KPI Targets to Track Daily

- Impressions
- Clicks
- CTR
- Indexed pages
- New business submissions

## Important Reality Check

3k-5k organic visits in 4-5 days is aggressive and not guaranteed. This sprint increases your probability by improving crawlability, indexing speed, intent targeting, and CTR quickly.
